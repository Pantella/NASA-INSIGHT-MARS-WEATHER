WHAT'S THE WEATHER LIKE ON MARS?

If you're curious about what's the weather like on Mars you're in the right place, thanks to InSight. NASA's InSight Mars lander takes continuous weather measurements on the surface of Mars at Elysium Planitia, a flat, smooth plain near Mars' equator.

By clicking on the button below you can access to temperature mesurement of current day and back to the last three days.

Please, note that data might not be available due to InSight needing to menage power use or temporarily Mars bad weather conditions.
